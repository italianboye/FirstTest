The History of Packing Peanuts

Packing peanuts, also known as foam peanuts or packing noodles,
are small, foam pieces used to fill empty spaces in shipping boxes
to protect fragile items during transit. Their history dates back to the mid-20th century.

Origins

Packing peanuts were first introduced in the 1960s as an alternative to traditional packing materials like newspaper and straw.
They were made from polystyrene foam, which provided excellent cushioning properties while being lightweight.
